# Commercial Pilot Exit Checklist

Use this checklist whether the pilot proceeds, pauses, or ends.

- [ ] Stop new pilot exercises at the agreed time.
- [ ] Identify open operational records and assign an approved disposition.
- [ ] Separate and inventory synthetic records without using destructive reset endpoints.
- [ ] Confirm real operational records, accepted snapshots, imports, audit events, and evidence remain preserved.
- [ ] Review unresolved feedback, incidents, blockers, and conditions.
- [ ] Export or retain only information allowed by the approved data-retention procedure.
- [ ] Remove or disable pilot user access through existing account governance where required.
- [ ] Confirm backups and rollback references remain available for the required retention period.
- [ ] Record the final agency and Platform decision, owner, date, and conditions.
- [ ] Confirm no provider credentials, secrets, passenger data, or raw logs remain in feedback or pilot reports.

Do not delete an agency, reset production, mutate history, or remove evidence as part of routine pilot exit. Follow approved production and data-governance procedures.
