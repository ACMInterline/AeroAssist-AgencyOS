# Demo Workspace Guide

The onboarding demo workspace contains synthetic, deterministic, tenant-owned records. It lets pilot staff explore normal, conditional, blocked, document-heavy, disruption, finance, and after-sales scenarios without contacting a provider or creating real travel.

## Profiles

- **Small Agency:** compact daily workload and common passenger-service examples.
- **Medium Agency:** broader team ownership, queue, and deadline context.
- **Corporate Agency:** business travel, approvals, documentation, and servicing examples.
- **Luxury Leisure Agency:** complex itineraries and higher-touch service examples.

## Safe Use

- Confirm records are marked synthetic before using them in exercises.
- Follow links from Operations rather than searching by copied IDs.
- Test expected validation failures and recovery paths.
- Safe regeneration is idempotent and reuses stable identifiers.
- Do not replace synthetic names with real passenger, credential, payment, or document data.
- Do not interpret PNR, ticket, EMD, payment, message, or provider fields as executed external actions.

Record gaps through **Pilot Help & Feedback**. If a record appears cross-tenant or cannot be distinguished from production work, stop and use the incident process.
