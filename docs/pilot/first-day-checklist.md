# Commercial Pilot First-Day Checklist

## Administrator

- [ ] Confirm the approved pilot environment, release reference, and Phase 57 human sign-off.
- [ ] Confirm health and public readiness report ready.
- [ ] Confirm agency name, time zone, currency, working hours, and branding.
- [ ] Confirm staff accounts and existing roles.
- [ ] Confirm the selected demo profile and synthetic workspace summary.
- [ ] Explain unsupported provider, payment, ticketing, messaging, and automation boundaries.
- [ ] Explain incident, feedback, backup, and recovery responsibilities.

## Travel Consultant

- [ ] Open Operations and identify urgent, overdue, blocked, and unassigned items.
- [ ] Open one linked Client and Passenger.
- [ ] Follow one Request through Trip and Offer context.
- [ ] Review one accepted-offer and booking handoff.
- [ ] Review Booking, Ticket/EMD, passenger-service, Document, Finance, and after-sales examples.
- [ ] Complete one safe queue action and verify its history.
- [ ] Submit one non-sensitive pilot feedback item and confirm it appears in Agency history.

## Platform Reviewer

- [ ] Open Commercial Pilot readiness and review blockers/warnings.
- [ ] Confirm Phase 57 governance is shown as preserved.
- [ ] Open Pilot Feedback Review and confirm tenant, context, and lifecycle controls.
- [ ] Record no production or release approval solely from this checklist.
