# Daily Operations Checklist

## Start of Day

- [ ] Confirm health/readiness is available through the approved monitoring procedure.
- [ ] Review urgent, overdue, due-soon, blocked, and unassigned work.
- [ ] Review departures and travel-critical documents/services.
- [ ] Review work waiting for client, airline/supplier, approval, documents, or payment.
- [ ] Assign clear owners and due dates.

## During the Day

- [ ] Work from linked records rather than copying identifiers into unrelated notes.
- [ ] Preserve accepted-offer and imported source snapshots.
- [ ] Keep internal instructions separate from client-facing messages.
- [ ] Record warnings and manual-review decisions rather than inventing certainty.
- [ ] Confirm every guarded transition reflects a real human decision.
- [ ] Submit non-sensitive feedback for defects, friction, data gaps, or documentation gaps.

## End of Day

- [ ] Recheck urgent, overdue, and unassigned queues.
- [ ] Record reasons for unresolved blockers and handovers.
- [ ] Confirm no synthetic scenario was mistaken for real operational work.
- [ ] Review incidents and high/urgent feedback with the Agency Administrator.
- [ ] Follow the approved operational backup process; do not trigger backup from Agency UI.
