# Commercial Pilot Administrator Guide

## Administrator Responsibilities

The Agency Owner or Administrator owns agency configuration, staff access, safe pilot scope, and acceptance evidence. Platform governance remains separate and must not be treated as agency operating authority.

## Setup and Access

- Review agency identity, branding, working hours, time zone, currency, and communication readiness.
- Invite only required staff using existing Agency roles. Do not invent roles or share credentials.
- Confirm read-only users cannot perform feedback or operational mutations.
- Review dashboard and notification preferences with the operating team.
- Keep synthetic demo records clearly separated from real client work.

## Daily Oversight

- Open Operations and review urgent, overdue, unassigned, and blocked work.
- Check document, approval, payment, passenger-service, and supplier-waiting conditions.
- Confirm assignments and due dates have clear owners.
- Review Agency feedback status and add new feedback when a pilot issue materially affects work.

## Safety

Never use a UI status as proof that an airline, supplier, payment provider, GDS, ticket, EMD, or external message action occurred. Preserve accepted-offer snapshots and imported source truth. Escalate cross-agency visibility, permission bypass, data loss, health/readiness failure, or unexplained state mutation immediately.

Use the [Incident reporting guide](incident-reporting-guide.md) and [Backup and recovery guide](backup-and-recovery-guide.md) for operational exceptions.
