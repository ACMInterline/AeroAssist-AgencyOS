# Supplementary Blueprint Adoption Map

Phase 36.4.5 adopts useful structures from the supplementary blueprint without replacing AgencyOS architecture. Phase 36.4.6 extends that alignment to standalone manual, imported confirmation/GDS, and existing-trip change/exchange workflows. Phase 36.5 adds the safe document foundation layer. Phase 36.6 adds governed GDS parser profiles, versions, runs, entities, corrections, samples, and evaluations. Phase 36.7 adds governed airline policy source ingestion, deterministic candidate extraction, human review, and approved knowledge records. Phase 36.8 adds canonical special/ancillary service taxonomy mapping for airline terms, SSR/GDS codes, and policy candidates. Phase 36.9 adds separate SSR/OSI communication mechanics and EMD/RFIC/RFISC payment mechanics mapping. Phase 37.0 adds ancillary pricing schema, pricing matrices, quote scenarios/results, and expanded service exception metadata. Phase 37.1 adds metadata-only airline policy comparison and service advisor views. Phase 37.2 links those advisor views into offer workspaces as metadata-only context. Phase 37.3 consumes saved advisor evidence into human-reviewed offer decision packs. Phase 37.4 records human explanations and decision timelines for those packs. Phase 37.5 records metadata-only decision export snapshots for review evidence. Phase 37.6 records metadata-only render previews for those exports. Phase 37.7 records metadata-only approvals and manual release readiness for those previews. Phase 37.8 records metadata-only manual delivery handoff workspaces for those approved exports. Phase 37.9 records metadata-only manual delivery outcomes after humans act outside AgencyOS. Phase 38.0 records metadata-only audit reviews over lifecycle completeness, approval trails, handoff trails, outcome trails, unresolved issues, and immutable snapshot coverage. Phase 38.1 records metadata-only export governance over rules, retention policy metadata, legal basis metadata, archive status metadata, exceptions, and immutable governance snapshots. `/platform/*` and `/agency/*` remain canonical, provider execution stays disabled, and existing request/trip/offer/booking/ticket/EMD models remain the source of truth.
